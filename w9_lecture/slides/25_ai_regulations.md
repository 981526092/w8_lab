# AI Regulations Worldwide

## Key regulations we'll look at in detail:
- **EU AI Act** — most comprehensive, risk-based framework (see next slides)
- **NYC Local Law 144** — first US law regulating AI in hiring (see next slides)

## Other notable regulations:
- **US:** Executive Order on AI Safety (2023)
- **UK:** AI Safety Institute; upcoming AI Bill
- **China:** Deep Synthesis Regulation; Generative AI Measures
- **Canada:** Artificial Intelligence and Data Act (AIDA)

## Key observation:
- Different jurisdictions take different approaches: **risk-based** (EU), **sector-specific** (US), **principles-based** (UK)
- No global standard yet — companies operating internationally must navigate **multiple overlapping frameworks**
