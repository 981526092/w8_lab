# Intended Learning Outcomes

## Part 0: Mechanistic Interpretability (continued from Week 8)
- Understand modern approaches to interpreting neural network internals
- Key concepts: linear probes, superposition, sparse autoencoders, activation patching
- Hands-on: Neuronpedia as an interactive tool

## Part 1: Human-in-the-Loop AI/ML
- What: When and where could/should humans be a part of the ML pipeline?
- Why: Contribute to improved model and human performance/efficiency
- Categories: Active Learning, Interactive ML, Machine Teaching

## Part 2: AI Auditing, Governance & Bias
- AI Auditing: Independent risk-based evaluation of AI systems
- AI Governance: EU AI Act, NYC Local Law 144, and global regulation
- AI Bias: Fairness definitions, bias in LLMs, detection & mitigation
