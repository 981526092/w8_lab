# Algorithm Audits

## Definition:
- A process of **independent and impartial system evaluation**, whereby an external auditor assesses an AI system's efficacy, risk levels, and vulnerabilities and offers recommendations to mitigate risks to an acceptable level

- A new industry to **professionalise and industrialise** AI (incl. ML and associated algorithms) from legal, ethical and safety standpoints

## Types of algorithm audits:
- **Model Audits:** Assess the results generated by an algorithm for Efficacy, Robustness, Fairness, Transparency and Privacy
- **Governance Audits:** Assess an AI developer's organisational procedures, accountability structures, oversight mechanisms and quality management systems

> Reference: Casper et al. (2024), https://doi.org/10.1098/rsos.230859
