# Why Do We Need to Audit AI?

- AI is being built, adopted and deployed at an **exponential rate**
- Widespread concerns about whether it/the companies developing it can be trusted because **unmanaged AI risks** are threatening society and causing major financial and reputational damage

## Notable examples:
- **Tay.ai:** Twitter taught Microsoft's AI chatbot to be racist in less than a day
- **Google Bard:** A factual error by Bard AI chatbot cost Google $100 billion
- **Amazon Recruiting:** Amazon scrapped sexist AI hiring tool
- **Facial Recognition:** Police tools found to be 'staggeringly inaccurate'
