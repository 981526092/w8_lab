# Missing Link

- The auditing and regulatory bodies **don't seem to provide equal importance** to usability and usefulness, right?

- Where is the link between the protection of human rights/societal values that these different regulatory bodies are working so hard to ensure, and providing accessibility and equal access in the first place?

## Concrete examples of the gap:
- **Digital divide:** GPT-4 requires $20/month — pricing creates unequal access to AI capabilities
- **Language gap:** Most AI tools optimised for English; EU AI Act doesn't mandate multilingual accessibility
- **Disability access:** Screen readers struggle with many AI interfaces; no regulation requires AI accessibility standards (cf. WCAG for web)

Regulation protects people *from* AI harms — but who ensures equal access *to* AI benefits?

- For now, it remains in the hands of developers to **build and deploy AI responsibly**
